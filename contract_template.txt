3. REVISION POLICY
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  • Revisions are defined as change requests made during the active build
    phase, before final delivery. They do not apply to post-delivery work.
  • Two (2) rounds of revisions are included in every project.
  • A revision round is defined as one consolidated set of feedback.
  • Additional revision rounds beyond the two included are billed at
    $25 per round, invoiced and due before work resumes.
  • Revisions do not include new features or changes to original scope.
    Those are treated as a new project and quoted separately.
  • Post-delivery support, bug fixes, content updates, and maintenance
    are not revisions — they are covered under the monthly retainer
    (see Section 2). Clients on the $200/month retainer receive unlimited
    ongoing maintenance, bug fixes, content updates, and technical support
    with no additional charge after delivery.

5. TERMINATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

    the full current month. No partial refunds are issued.
